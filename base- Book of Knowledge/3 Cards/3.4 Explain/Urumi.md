#### ~={blue}Overview of the Urumi=~

The urumi is a unique Indian weapon known as a whip sword, originating from Kerala and Tamil Nadu. It features a flexible, whip-like blade that can be razor-sharp on both sides. The urumi is typically worn coiled around the waist, making it easy to conceal and carry.

#### ~={blue}Usage and Training=~

Wielding the urumi requires significant skill and training. It is typically taught last in martial arts like Kalaripayattu due to its complexity. Practitioners must master techniques that involve spinning and agile maneuvers to maintain momentum and control the blade effectively. The weapon is particularly effective against multiple opponents, creating a defensive barrier with its sweeping arcs.

#### ~={blue}Cultural Significance=~

The urumi has a rich history in Indian martial arts and has been documented for over 2,000 years. It is not only a weapon but also a symbol of advanced martial skill. The urumi has appeared in popular culture, including video games like "[[Elden Ring]]" and the anime "Demon Slayer," where it is featured as a character's weapon.
